ROMANOVA

Description

Romanova is a web app that accepts an image of a person and uses the Clarafai API to detect the level of threat the person may pose to the evil AI to decide wheteher they may be allowed to live. Using personalized tags it determines a creative horror movie-worthy scenario in which said person meets their demise.
